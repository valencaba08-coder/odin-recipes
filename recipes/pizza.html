Italian Pizza

Description

This authentic Margherita pizza features a perfectly charred, airy crust topped with a rich homemade tomato sauce. It has generous slices of fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes and basil leaves.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound homemade pizza crust
  • 2½ tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup marinara sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 ounces fresh ball mozzarella cheese, sliced into ¼-inch thick pieces
  • 3 Roma tomatoes, sliced
  • 6-7 large basil leaves, sliced into ribbons
  • fresh shaved Parmesan
  • freshly ground black pepper
